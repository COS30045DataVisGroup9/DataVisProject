Australian migration flow from countries all over the world from 1980 to 2008

Year: 2000

The visualisation describes the migration flow from and to Australia in the 1980 - 2008 period. Migration flow consists of both inflow (immigration) and outflow (emigration).

  • Hover on a country to see the migration flow trend of that country from 1980 to 2008.
  • Swipe left or right of the slider above the map to see the migration flow in different years. Zoom in the map to see small regions.

Australia overseas immigration and emigration by visa groupings in 2021

The visualisation describes the number of immigrants and emigrants categorised by visa groups in 2021. Based on the types of visa, we can analyse the main reasons that people have been coming to and leaving from Australia.

  • Hover on a category to see the number of migrants belonging to that category

Net overseas migration of selected countries by Australia states in 2021

The visualisation show net overseas migration value of ten countries categorised by Australia states in 2021. Net overseas migration (NOM) refers to the net increase or decrease in population resulting from the inflow of immigrants to Australia and the outflow of emigrants from Australia. NOM is calculated by subtracting the total number of permanent and long-term departures from the total number of permanent and long-term arrivals.

  • Hover on a link to see the exact NOM value
  • Zoom in or move the node on two sides to see thin links
